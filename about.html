Our story

We launched in 2016 as a platform for hiring artisans and semi-skilled workers. We were sharing happiness with the artisans and the clients but we looked stressed for the most part.
In 2017, we pivoted to a digital services-only freelance platform where users could find, connect with, hire and pay diversely skilled digital professionals. We offered a secured escrow service and our users loved it.

Currently, we build software products and extend the engineering capacities of ambitious companies.
Our mission is to empower today’s companies and workers with the technology and opportunities needed to succeed in the evolving digital economy.
By software products, we mean desktop apps, web portals, mobile apps, animations, graphic designs, copywriting and more.
We have a tested and reliable project management approach that combines the best of agile and scrum on our products. From start to finish, we ensure that the development experience is as satisfying as the completed product.

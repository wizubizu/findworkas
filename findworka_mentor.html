About Findworka Mentors

Findworka Mentors is a network of experienced programmers and technical leaders who are willing to train and mentor the next generation of aspiring programmers.
The need for competent developers is increasing everyday. This surge in popularity is driven by the demands of today's internet users for dynamic and applications, built with today’s users in mind.
We know the role that quality tutoring and mentoring can play in the development of an ambitious developer. That’s why we are extending an open invitation to anyone who is qualified to help this new generation of beginners.
To be accepted as a Findworka Mentor, you need to have at least five years of software development experience. You must have collaborated with different teams, a track record of working on successful development projects and most importantly be willing to share your experience with upcoming programming talents.
